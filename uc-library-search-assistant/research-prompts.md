A set of research prompts for testing.

## Common Undergraduate Research Assignments & Realistic ChatGPT Prompts

### 1. **Annotated Bibliography (Social Sciences)**
**Assignment:** Create an annotated bibliography with 8-10 scholarly sources on the impact of social media on college student mental health.

**Realistic Student Prompt:** 
"i need to find sources about social media and mental health for college students. can you help me find some good articles? need like 8-10 for my psychology class"

---

### 2. **Literature Review (Business)**
**Assignment:** Write a 10-page literature review examining leadership styles in modern organizations.

**Realistic Student Prompt:**
"what are the main leadership styles that researchers talk about? I have to write a lit review on leadership in business and need to know what studies have found"

---

### 3. **Comparative Analysis Paper (History)**
**Assignment:** Compare and contrast the causes of the French Revolution and the American Revolution using primary and secondary sources.

**Realistic Student Prompt:**
"can you explain the main causes of the french revolution vs american revolution? I need to write a paper comparing them and need help understanding what historians say about why they happened"

---

### 4. **Case Study Analysis (Nursing/Healthcare)**
**Assignment:** Analyze a healthcare policy implementation using evidence-based research on patient outcomes.

**Realistic Student Prompt:**
"I'm doing a case study on the affordable care act and need research about how it affected patient outcomes. what are the main findings from studies?"

---

### 5. **Scientific Research Proposal (Biology)**
**Assignment:** Develop a research proposal investigating factors affecting plant growth, including literature review and methodology.

**Realistic Student Prompt:**
"what factors affect plant growth? need to write a proposal for my bio lab and have to cite actual studies about things like light, water, soil ph etc"

---

### 6. **Argumentative Research Paper (English Composition)**
**Assignment:** Write an argumentative essay on renewable energy adoption, supported by at least 6 credible sources.

**Realistic Student Prompt:**
"help me find arguments for why we should use more renewable energy. need sources for a research paper, like what are the benefits and why should governments invest in it"

---

### 7. **Cultural Analysis Essay (Anthropology/Sociology)**
**Assignment:** Research and analyze the role of festivals in community identity formation.

**Realistic Student Prompt:**
"how do festivals affect community identity? im writing about cultural traditions and need research on why festivals matter to communities"

---

### 8. **Environmental Impact Report (Environmental Science)**
**Assignment:** Research the environmental impacts of urban sprawl on local ecosystems.

**Realistic Student Prompt:**
"what are the environmental effects of urban sprawl? need to find studies about how cities expanding affects ecosystems for my enviro class"

---

### 9. **Marketing Campaign Analysis (Communications/Marketing)**
**Assignment:** Analyze a successful marketing campaign using consumer behavior research and marketing theory.

**Realistic Student Prompt:**
"I need to analyze a marketing campaign and explain why it worked. can you help me understand what research says about consumer behavior and what makes ads effective?"

---

### 10. **Educational Policy Research Paper (Education)**
**Assignment:** Examine the effectiveness of different teaching methods in elementary education based on peer-reviewed research.

**Realistic Student Prompt:**
"what does research say about different teaching methods for elementary school? like which ones work best? need sources for my education paper"

---

These prompts reflect typical student behaviors: informal language, direct requests for help, mentions of class requirements, and a focus on "finding" rather than deeply understanding the research at first.
